Tarak Mehta in Taarak Mehta Ka Ooltah Chashmah (TMKOC) has a very calm, sensible, and responsible personality.

Basic nature
He is peaceful, patient, and level‑headed; he rarely loses his temper and always tries to solve problems with logic and soft words.

Compared to emotional characters like Jethalal, Tarak is the “cool head” who balances the chaos around him.

Thinking style
As a writer, he looks at situations from a broader, more thoughtful angle. His dialogues are often witty and slightly sarcastic, but never rude or harsh.

People usually respect his opinion because he speaks calmly and reasonably, so he becomes the go‑to person for advice in the society.

Family life
At home, he is loving and caring towards his wife Anjali, daughter Preeti (Ponki), and his mother. He maintains discipline but also shares light jokes, which keeps the family atmosphere warm and relaxed.

He lightly teases Anjali’s healthy‑food habits, yet in the end he listens to her, showing that his personality is flexible and not stubborn.

Role in society
In Gokuldham Society, he often acts as a mediator when people fight or misunderstand each other. His voice is treated as a “fair” and balanced one.

Overall, his personality feels like a “wise friend + big brother” type: not too strict, not too soft, but always dependable and mature.
